Our System

We specialise in installing BIS-approved and globally certified fire protection equipment to ensure maximum reliability and compliance for your assets.

Fire Safety Systems We Install

Whatever your system needs, we design and install complete fire safety solutions tailored to your building – from detection and alarms to suppression, hydrants and sprinklers.

Fire Hydrant & Sprinkler Systems

We design and install complete fire hydrant lines, wet risers and sprinkler networks to protect large industrial, commercial and residential complexes.

  • Hydrant mains, landing valves, hose reels and hose boxes
  • Automatic sprinkler networks for coverage of critical areas
  • Design as per local fire norms and relevant standards

Intelligent Fire Detection & Alarm Systems

Early and reliable detection is the core of any fire safety strategy. We install intelligent addressable and conventional systems for rapid warning and response.

  • Addressable / conventional fire alarm panels
  • Smoke, heat and multi-sensor detectors
  • Manual call points, hooters and sounders

Fire Pump Sets & Water Storage

For hydrant and sprinkler systems to perform, reliable water supply and pressure are essential. We install complete fire pump rooms and associated piping.

  • Main, standby and jockey pumps with controllers
  • Connection to underground / overhead fire tanks
  • Testing, commissioning and performance checks

Gas & Special Hazard Suppression Systems

For electrical rooms, server areas and critical assets, we provide clean agent and other special suppression systems that control fire without damaging equipment.

  • Clean agent systems (FM-200 / inert gas)
  • CO2 systems for specific hazards
  • Integration with detection and alarm controls

Foam & High-Risk Area Protection

For fuel storage, loading bays and other high-risk zones, foam systems offer fast coverage and control over flammable liquid fires.

  • Foam pourers, generators and proportioning systems
  • Protection for tanks, bays and process areas
  • Design tuned to risk category and standards
