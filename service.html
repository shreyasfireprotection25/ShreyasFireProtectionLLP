Specialized Fire Safety Systems

Fire Detection & Alarm

Immediate and reliable threat identification is crucial. We install state-of-the-art systems to alert occupants and trigger response protocols instantly.

  • Addressable Fire Alarm Panels
  • Smoke, Heat, and Gas Detectors
  • Manual Call Points & Hooters

Fire Suppression Systems

Protecting critical assets and infrastructure requires fast, automated suppression tailored to the hazard.

  • CO2 and FM200 Gas Flooding Systems
  • Modular and Standalone Suppressors
  • Kitchen Fire Suppression Solutions

Fire Fighting Systems

Robust, reliable, and compliant water-based systems form the backbone of any large-scale protection strategy.

  • Fire Hydrant and Sprinkler Systems
  • Wet Riser / Dry Riser Installations
  • Fire Pumps and Water Storage Tanks

Integrated Fire Combat Equipment

Fire Hydrant System

A robust network of pipes, risers, and hydrants/hose reels providing pressurized water for manual fire fighting. Essential for large industrial and commercial complexes.

Fire Sprinkler System

Automatic detection and suppression via temperature-sensitive sprinkler heads. Our systems are compliant with NFPA and local standards for rapid fire control.

Fire Pump Sets

Diesel and Electric Pump sets designed to maintain high pressure and water flow for sprinkler and hydrant systems. Includes main, standby, and jockey pumps.

Fire Alarm System

Addressable and conventional fire alarm panels connected to smoke, heat detectors, and manual call points for early warning and rapid response protocol initiation.

Auxiliary Safety & Evacuation

Portable Fire Extinguishers

The first line of defense against minor fires. We provide a full range of high-quality portable units certified for various fire classes (A, B, C, D, K).

  • Dry Powder (ABC) and Water-based
  • CO2 for electrical and liquid fires
  • Foam and Wet Chemical extinguishers
  • Annual refilling and maintenance services

Public Address & Voice Alarm

Critical for mass evacuation. Our integrated Public Address (PA) and Voice Alarm (VA) systems ensure clear, timely, and unambiguous communication during emergencies.

  • Automatic and manual message broadcasting
  • Zoning and clear intelligibility standards
  • Battery backup for continuous operation

Fire & Safety Signboards

Ensure personnel safety and regulatory compliance with clear, durable, and visible signage.

Types of Signage Available:

  • Exit/Egress Signs (Photoluminescent)
  • Fire Extinguisher Location
  • Mandatory/Prohibition Signs
  • Assembly Point Markers
