OUR TEAM

The people behind your fire safety.

Shreyas Enterprises is led by a team of experienced fire safety professionals with over four decades of hands-on experience in fire protection and safety management. Under the leadership of Mr. Shashidhara Bangera, our engineers, designers, supervisors and technicians work with a single vision: to protect lives and property with reliable, standards-compliant fire systems.

“Our team brings together young, enthusiastic engineers and seasoned professionals. From system design and installation to maintenance and approvals, we cover every aspect of fire protection under one roof.”

Proprietor • 40+ Years Experience

Mr. Shashidhara Bangera

Founder and Proprietor of Shreyas Enterprises, Mr. Shashidhara Bangera has over 40 years of unmatched experience in Fire Protection & Safety Management. Before starting his own organization in 2005, he led professional teams for renowned companies such as Monsher Fire Inc. and Nitin Fire Protection & Services.

Under his leadership, Shreyas Enterprises delivers complete fire fighting solutions: design, installation, maintenance, hydrant systems, sprinklers, fire detection and suppression, and turnkey projects—all under one roof.

General Manager • 20 Years Experience

Mr. Ravichandra Poojary

As General Manager with 15 years of experience, Mr. Ravichandra Poojary oversees project execution, coordination with clients, and overall operational efficiency across sites.

His deep understanding of fire fighting systems and on-site challenges ensures that each project is delivered safely, on schedule, and in line with regulatory standards.
